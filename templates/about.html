About Me

Innovating Through Technology and Education

Hello, I'm Amitesh Jha, a B.Tech student specializing in Computer Science Engineering at CCSIT, TMU, and a passionate educator with a strong inclination toward technology and innovation.

As a dedicated online educator, I have successfully taught Physics to CBSE students up to Class 12 for five years, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic, ensuring quality education even during challenging times. My YouTube channel, Educaation Arena, has become a trusted resource for students, featuring tutorials on Python, Data Structures, and foundational computer science topics.

Key Achievements and Projects:

  • I Reserve - Seat Booking App: Developed a Flask-based seat booking system with features like wing selection, time slots, and email/SMS notifications, providing a seamless booking experience.
  • Python Tutorials: Created comprehensive series on NumPy, Flask, and Tkinter to help students build real-world projects.
  • Research Publication: Successfully published a research paper titled "Web 2.0 vs Web 3.0: A Comparative Analysis," contributing to the understanding of emerging web technologies.

Beyond academics, I have consistently focused on self-improvement and growth, completing a rigorous course in C++ and Data Structures from Coding Ninjas, where I solved 100 questions in just 21 days. I am also involved in creating responsive portfolios and user-friendly web applications.

Join me on this journey to explore the endless possibilities of technology and education. Together, let's innovate, learn, and create a better future!
